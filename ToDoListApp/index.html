        features

        First, what is a To-do list?
        According to the name, To-do list are lists and tasks you want to do and you won't want to forget easily. They are a lots of works in someone heads. But this my To-do list is divided into three(3) part which are, uncomplete list, complete List and hidden list.

        • Uncomplete List:This is where the tasks you haven't completed are. It is the main to-do list.
        • List: This is where the tasks you have completed are. It is below the to-do List.
        • hidden List:This is where the tasks you didn't want to share with anybody are. like, surprises, buy birthday gift, promises, something special and more.
        you can also set a reminder for your to-do task but you can only set one reminder.
